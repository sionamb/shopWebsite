About Us

The Wood'n Spoon features the bountiful products of Michigan's Keweenaw Peninsula, such as handmade natural preserves, jams, jellies and syrups, naturally grown produce, and baked goods. Many of the wild berries are picked in the Keweenaw forests. We grow the berries for our preserves and our fresh produce. On our 70 acre farm, we use no chemical pesticides and we use as many natural practices as possible. In addition to these products, we offer many local specialties including native copper, agates, datolite, books about the Upper Peninsula, post cards, "T"-Shirts, maple syrup, fruit cakes, and honey.

Products

Our shop specializes in making jams, jellies, berry syrups, gourmet cookies, and home-style breads. We grow the berries for our preserves, along with fresh produce sold seasonally (strawberry, vegetables, etc.) on our 70 acre farm, where we use no chemical pesticides and as many natural practices as possible. We also put the bounty of the Keweenaw to good use by using many wild berries in our products (Thimbleberry Walnut Cookies, jams, jellies, etc.). The wild berries are picked in the magnificent woods of the Keweenaw.
